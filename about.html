ABOUT ME

Welcome to WM Nailbar, a home-based nail studio created with passion, creativity, and a love for self-expression. WM Nailbar was built on the idea that nails are more than just beauty — they are a reflection of your personality, mood, and style. Whether you’re feeling soft and elegant, bold and dramatic, trendy and playful, or clean and minimal, this is a space that caters to every vibe.
As a home-based studio, WM Nailbar offers a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere where clients can unwind and enjoy a personalized experience. Every appointment is focused on you — your preferences, your inspiration, and your vision. We take the time to understand exactly what you want and bring it to life with precision and attention to detail.
Quality and hygiene are top priorities at WM Nailbar. We use professional products and follow proper sanitation practices to ensure every service is not only beautiful but safe and long-lasting. From classic sets to custom nail art, each design is done with care, patience, and creativity.
Our mission is simple: to provide a welcoming space where everyone feels confident, valued, and empowered. When you leave WM Nailbar, you don’t just leave with great nails — you leave feeling polished, confident, and ready to take on the world.
