Services for Every Ocassion

MANICURE

Nails cut, filed and shaped. Cuticles pushed back and tidied, and then enjoy a hand massage. Finally, nails painted with a colour of your choice.

PEDICURE

Dead skin and cells rubbed off the bottom of the feet using a rough stone. Skincare provided up to the knee, including granular exfoliation. moisturizing and massage.

NAIL EXTENSIONS

Use of UV Gel to attach pre-made nails of customers choice

ACRYLIC NAILS

Created with liquid monomer and power polymer. Mixture is applied onto nails and brushed towards the tip. Nails are then left to dry for 10 minutes before polishing.
